Bar & Events for 2020 


PHCC has a modest bar, available to members and their g​uests. 

As with all club undertakings, the bar is run by unpaid volunteer members. A consequence of this arrangement allows the provision of competitively priced soft drinks, bottled beers, wines, spirits and snacks

The bar is open between 12.00 & 15.00 most days of the week, Tuesday evenings 20.00
-
23.59 (“Club Night”)
and on occasions of special club events.
Light hearted banter is the norm but exchange of useful hints and tips between members is also prevalent.
